Liv - De Molens: "Living on the Leie in Deinze"

"Liv- De Molens" is a residential housing project, located along the Leie and in the middle of a large park in Deinze. With a perfectly balanced combination of spacious, high-quality apartments, space for relaxation and greenery, shops, offices and urban facilities, this project on the Leie promises to become the largest attraction for Deinze and the surrounding area. Architecture of the absolute best in the world by Reiulf Ramstad Architects. A site of 2.2 hectares on the Leie, in the immediate vicinity shops, schools, station, public transport, good connection to roads. In Liv © De Molens you will find some historic buildings that fit in nicely with the stylish new building. The ultimate mix of authentic charm and trendy living. This combination gives the site extraordinary character: a harmonious place that radiates tranquility and beauty.
